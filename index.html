Publications

SK-SVR-CNN: A Hybrid Approach for Traffic Flow Prediction with Signature PDE Kernel and Convolutional Neural Networks

Gezhi Wang, Zepu Wang, Peng Sun, Azzedine Boukerche

IEEE International Conference on Communications (ICC), 2024

Developed a novel hybrid approach combining Signature PDE Kernel with Support Vector Regression (SVR) and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for accurate traffic flow prediction. The method leverages the mathematical properties of signature kernels to capture temporal dependencies in traffic data, while CNN extracts spatial features from traffic network topology.

Key Results:

  • Improved prediction accuracy compared to traditional methods
  • Enhanced handling of complex traffic patterns and temporal dependencies
  • Robust performance across different traffic network configurations
  • Efficient computational approach for real-time traffic management
